Empathy, what is it?

Entering the UI/UX space, I am often met with the term 'Empathy' or 'User Centered Design'. I always thought that we could design great products only by passion and grit, but I later came to realise that the successful products we create are ones that provide real value to a user. And to provide real value to a user, we have to understand our users and the necessities of the human condition. In this book, I came to learn about the power of empathy and how it can greatly facilitate the UI/UX design process when it comes to understanding the needs of our users.

Customer Experience is now a key competitive differentiator; however, to truly stand out, organizations need to have and apply empathy for their customers. This wonderfully insightful book teaches us why empathy is important, how to gain it, and how to apply it within our businesses.
– Ricard Dalton, AVP Experience Design, USAA
